    This year we're trying something a bit new -- allowing virtual audience members to come on Webcam, and be broadcast LIVE into the room. So whether you want to come on camera and actually ask a question, or simply want to have your live video presence seen in the room (without asking a question), here's what you need to know:

    • Click the Turn On Webcam button to start your video
    • Once you're broadcasting, if you'd like to ask a question, click to "Raise Hand" (make sure you're wearing headphones)
    • If called on, you will see a large prompt that you are "LIVE in the room" - start speaking
    • Note: this is a bit of an experiment, so we likely won't use this component for every presentation
